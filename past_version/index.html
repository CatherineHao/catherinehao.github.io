Biography

Hello, I am Jianing Hao (Catherine), a second-year Ph.D. student in Data Science and Analytics (DSA) at The Hong Kong University of Science and Technology (GZ), supervised by Prof. Wei Zeng, and Prof. Guang Zhang. I received my B.Eng degree in Computer Science and Technology in Shandong University in 2022, supervised by Prof. Zhaohui Peng.
My research interest is centered around the interplay of Human Intelligence Artificial Intelligence, with a particular interest in Time-series data, Finance and XAI.
